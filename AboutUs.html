Our Company - Meals On Map

At Meals On Map, we're proud to have been connecting customers with the some of the nation's best cooks. Headquartered in Los Angeles, California, Meals On Map has over 13,000 member providers throughout the U.S. and Canada, with an additional 20,000 affiliated providers outside North America. This extensive network, coupled with our commitment to exceptional customer service, means that when you order meals from Meals On Map, you can be confident that you're receiving the best. As a service organization rather than an eat-out, Meals On Map makes it easy to select healthy home made food by seeking continuous innovation and improvement in services, and providing the best people in the area around you, to ensure your confidence that your order will be placed easily and filled by a Meals On Map provider quickly and professionally to your complete satisfaction.

Why Meals On Map is Different

Any restaurant in the localities pre-package their food and store them in low temerature, making it really risky to have an order delivered,finally which are loaded onto trucks and dropped at the recipient's door. Once they finally get inside, the food may be dehydrated and not fresh. But a Meals On Map order is different. At Meals On Map, we know that a truly exceptional order of food relies on the expertise of a everyday cook. All of our dishes/meals are hand cooked by a local provider using only the freshest vegetables available, and each order is personally taken care, to see that the customer has all his requiremrents satisfied, ready to be enjoyed immediately.
