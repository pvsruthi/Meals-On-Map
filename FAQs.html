How are the ordered dishes packaged?

Provider delivered orders
All provider delivered orders will arrive beautifully presented in food safe aluminium wrap or take away containers and presented in a bag.

Self Pick up
All Self Pick up orders can be filled into the boxes that the customers bring with them or are wrapped in food safe aluminium foils or take away containers.

Once you have placed your order, our expert cooks will lovingly cook with the care and attention it deserves.
