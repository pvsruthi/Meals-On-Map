Meals On Map - Your very own, almost MOM-made food available for you

Healthy and nutritious food is a basic need for every one of us. What could be better than a fresh, tasty and healthy homemade meal? We are introducing our unique application “Meals On the Map (MOM)” which will satisfy this very need of our people. The idea behind our application is to make fresh homemade food available to people in a neighborhood which is cooked by the chefs in the same neighborhood. Here we are not only satisfying food needs of people but also creating business opportunity for home based cooks.
